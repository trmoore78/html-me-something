Red Velvet Cheesecake Cake

For Valentine's Day, I made a heart-shaped Red Velvet Cheesecake Cake. One of my favorite desserts that my grandmother made for me was a Red Velvet Cake. So, of course, when I went to The Cheesecake Factory and saw a Red Velvet Cheesecake Cake, my mouth drooled. It combined two of the best desserts, Red Velvet Cake and Cheesecake. With that being said, the research began to see what all went into making the cheesecake. After much trial and error (a lot of trial and error), I now use the below recipe for it.

Ingredients

  • 2 -- 8 oz. packages of cream cheese
  • 2/3 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1/8 tsp of salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2/3 tbsp vanilla extract

After mixing all of the ingredients together, I pour the cheesecake in a greased pan that was lined with parchment paper. Then I place it in a â€œwater bath" and bake for about 55 minutes. The time would probably vary depending on how your oven cooks. The oven temperature that I use is 325 degrees.
